Department Laboratories

The Information Technology Department is equipped with well-structured labs supported by modern infrastructure, high-speed internet, and advanced systems for hands-on training. Students are provided with Core i5 laptops (for PG) and can access Wi-Fi on request.

Software Lab

In this lab, students are trained in computer languages, databases, web development tools, and academic projects. The lab accommodates 32 students simultaneously and is equipped with software like Turbo C++ Editor, Python, JDK1.8, Oracle 8i, Dreamweaver, NetBeans IDE, and Network Animator.

Hardware Lab

This lab focuses on data structures and computer hardware. It supports 32 students working simultaneously, each with individual laptops for hands-on practice.

Machine Learning Lab

Students practice networking and microprogramming in this lab, equipped with 32 systems having multimedia support. Software includes Keil IDE for 8051 and Packet Tracer, along with 28 microprocessor kits (8085).
