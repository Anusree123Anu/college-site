Admission

Admission Processes

A candidate who has successfully completed her Kerala engineering exam or KEAM exam can opt for the option for GEC Idukki for the department of Information technology through the site. Once the candidate is opted for the allotment process, he or she has to be physically available in the department for the rest of the process of the admission by filling a form that is released in the site every year by the official and uploading the required documents. After the physical verification the candidate is required to pay the fees he or she has to remit in the college.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The candidate must have completed and passed their 12th.
  • The candidate should have a valid KEAM score.
  • Must include physics and mathematics as major subjects and one optional subject as third.
  • Must be at least 17 years old.
  • Selection will be equally based on KEAM score and 12th marks.

Fees Structure

The fee structure for the B.Tech programs at Government Engineering College, Idukki (GEC Idukki) is prescribed by the Government of Kerala and follows the norms of the Commissioner for Entrance Examinations (CEE). Students admitted through KEAM are required to remit the tuition fee, special fees, and examination fees during admission. Being a government institution, the fee is comparatively affordable, ensuring quality education at a reasonable cost. Hostel fees, caution deposit, and university registration charges are collected separately as per the rules of the college. Any updates or revisions in the fee structure will be officially announced by the institution.
